Frosted Glass Layers

Multi-layered translucent panels with subtle depth via box shadows and gradient overlays. Uses SwiftUI's .ultraThinMaterial with custom tints.

Rounded Corners Everywhere

Number badges become rounded squares (8-12px radius) instead of diamonds. More cohesive with macOS design language. Avatar also becomes rounded rectangle.

Glowing Progress Bar

Seek bar gains a soft teal glow effect with a luminous knob. The fill uses a gradient from primary to light teal for visual interest.

Subtle Section Reveals

Sections animate in with spring physics + opacity. Search field has a frosted glass appearance matching the overall theme.

Teal Accent Glow

The now-playing area gains a subtle teal gradient wash at the top, creating a warm ambient glow that indicates active playback.

Playing State Animation

Currently playing surah gets a pulsing audio indicator icon with a soft glow, replacing the static speaker icon.

Direction B

Luminous Dark

Ultra-dark canvas with vibrant purple-indigo-teal gradients. Animated gradient progress bar, glowing accent orbs, and edge-lit active states. Bold and cinematic.

Animated Gradient Bar

The seek bar uses a shifting purple-indigo-teal gradient that slowly animates. Creates a living, breathing feel to the playback indicator.

Ambient Light Orbs

Soft radial gradients float behind the now-playing area, creating depth and atmosphere. Purple top-right, teal bottom-left.

Edge-Lit Active Row

Currently playing surah gets a vertical gradient accent bar on the left edge, transitioning from purple to teal. Eye-catching without being loud.

Animated Audio Bars

The playing indicator uses 3 animated bars (purple, indigo, teal) that bounce in rhythm, replacing the static speaker icon.

Pulsing Avatar Ring

Selected reciter's avatar gets a subtle pulsing border ring that breathes slowly, indicating active selection and adding life.

Tag-Style Metadata

Reciter name becomes a styled tag/badge. Recording counts use pill-shaped badges with accent background. More visual hierarchy.

Direction C

Sacred Gold

Rich dark canvas with warm gold accents inspired by traditional Islamic art. Octagonal geometry, ornamental patterns, and a reverent, premium feel befitting the Quran.

Islamic Geometric Patterns

Subtle repeating geometric patterns in the background of the now-playing area, inspired by traditional Islamic art. Very low opacity so it doesn't distract.

Octagonal Number Badges

Surah numbers use an 8-sided (octagonal) shape via CSS clip-path, referencing Islamic architectural motifs. Replaces the diamond shape.

Rounded Square Play Button

The play button uses a rounded square (squircle) instead of a circle, giving it a premium, app-icon feel. Gold gradient fill with warm shadow.

Warm Gold Typography

Arabic text uses warm white (#F5F0E0) instead of pure white, creating a parchment-like warmth. English text stays neutral for readability.

Ornamental Accents

Small decorative star ornaments (✦) appear as the playing indicator, referencing Islamic decorative tradition. Elegant and culturally resonant.

Gold Border Accents

Selected reciter avatars get a thin gold border. The overall container has a subtle gold-tinted border. Premium, jewelry-like feel.
